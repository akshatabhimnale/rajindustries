Cable harnesses for passenger vehicles From wheel arch to tailgate

WeWire, a brand of the Coroplast Group, works worldwide on creating innovative solutions for the automobile industry. With approx. 6,750 employees working at 15 production sites, we are never far away from our customers. Both the major automobile manufacturers and their Tier-1 suppliers rely on the broad range of wire harnesses that we provide. At the same time, these well-known customers appreciate our service philosophy and the wealth of problem-solving expertise that WeWire engineers have gained through many years of experience.
