## Product Focus: Stand-Alone Wire Harnesses

### Positioning

- Highly customized stand-alone harnesses for pre-assembled components
- Built to fit 100% with precise routing and application-specific protection
- Designed for OEMs and Tier suppliers with fast development and cost optimization

### Typical Applications

- Motors and generators
- Doors, roofs, headliners, tailgates
- Seats and seat systems
- HVAC and air‑handling units
- Shock absorbers and front-end modules

### What Customers Can Expect

- Deep experience in cable-harness manufacturing
- Collaboration in Simultaneous Engineering Teams (SETs)
- Development-based qualification and validation
- Wiring diagrams and component drawings
- 3D routing with collision detection (DMU)
- Functional models using 3D printing
- Interfaces to customer and supplier systems

### Technical Specifications

- Insulation materials: high‑quality materials including silicone
- Conductor materials: copper, aluminum
- Cross‑sections: 16–70 mm²
- Termination options: crimp terminals, tubular cable lugs, die‑cast contacts, battery clamps

### Specialized Harness Types

**Battery and Generator Cable Harnesses**

- Built for strict performance and safety requirements
- Designed for high-current paths and robust termination

**Engine Wiring Harnesses**

- Heat‑resistant single conductors up to 200°C
- Sealed connection systems and potential distributors
- Highly flexible cables for tight installation spaces
- Abrasion protection and optimized shielding

**Seat and Interior Wiring**

- Highly flexible single conductors
- Airbag wiring support
- Motion compensation using braided sleeving
- Abrasion‑protection tape and fastening systems
- Cable ducts and routing management
